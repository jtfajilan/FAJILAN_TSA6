Naruto Uzumaki

Age: 19

Village: Konohagakure (Hidden Leaf Village)

Rank: Genin (Part I), Hokage (Part II)

Naruto is a young ninja from the Hidden Leaf Village who dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village. He is known for his determination, strong sense of justice, and his ability to inspire others.

Sasuke Uchiha

Age: 19

Village: Konohagakure (Hidden Leaf Village)

Rank: Genin (Part I), Avenger (Part II)

Sasuke is a young ninja from the Uchiha clan who seeks revenge against his brother Itachi for killing their family. He is known for his cold and distant personality, as well as his exceptional fighting skills.

Jiraiya

Age: 51

Village: Konohagakure (Hidden Leaf Village)

Rank: Sannin (Three Legendary Sannin)

Jiraiya is a legendary ninja and one of the Three Legendary Sannin of the Hidden Leaf Village. He is known for his exceptional strength, wisdom, and perverted nature.

Gaara

Age: 18

Village: Sunagakure (Hidden Sand Village)

Rank: Fifth Kazekage

Gaara is the Fifth Kazekage of the Hidden Sand Village and the younger brother of Temari and Kankuro. He is known for his exceptional strength, his ability to control sand, and his cold and distant personality.

Rock Lee

Age: 17

Village: Konohagakure (Hidden Leaf Village)

Rank: Genin (Part I), Jonin (Part II)

Rock Lee is a young ninja from the Hidden Leaf Village who dreams of becoming a great ninja despite his lack of talent in ninjutsu. He is known for his strong sense of determination, his exceptional physical strength, and his comedic relief.
